Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also offer payment via PayPal for annual subscriptions.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades are effective immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

We offer special discounts for eligible non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team for more information and to see if you qualify.
